Where are we heading today?

On the web everything is connected – through links, buttons or images. But it takes someone to navigate through it and doing so, everyone differs.

For one day every group member collected the Call-To-Actions (CTA), in our case mostly buttons, on websites that he visited – visualising each individual journey through the tools used and interests pursued.

The process consists of taking a screenshot of any meaningful CTA on the site visited, linking to it the destination or URL where it originally was located and then trying to be as consistent as possible.
